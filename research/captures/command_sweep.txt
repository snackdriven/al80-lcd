Read-only command sweep on the 0xFF60 LCD interface:
- 0x55 (getDongleAndKeyboardStatus): returned FF 00 00... = NAK
- 0xB0 (getFirmwareVersion): returned FF 00 00... = NAK (tested earlier)
CONCLUSION: the 0xFF60 screen interface ONLY accepts screen-control cmds (0x40/0x41/0x42)
and NAKs everything else. Status/version/config commands must live on a different
interface (likely 0xFF31). Do NOT probe 0xB1-0xB7 (bootloader/DFU - brick risk).
